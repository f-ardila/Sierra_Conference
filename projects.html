Osterbrock Sierra Conference Projects

Newsletter

Following the 2019 conference, there was interest in starting a UC-wide periodic newsletter as a way to stay informed about university-wide news, updates at each department, upcoming events and conferences, student publications, and other relevant information. Check out the issues below.

Slack Workspace

Following the 2019 conference, we also started a UC-wide astro grads slack workspace to keep in touch with each other, plan events, advertise talk slots, discuss topics of interest, and share memes. Contact your local liaison to join!

Contact Us
